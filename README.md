The Daily Walk | 365 Bible Reading Plan
An interactive Bible reading plan created for New Christian Next Steps.
About
This plan guides you through the entire Bible in 365 days, organized into 40-day segments with daily readings from the Old Testament, a Gospel or Letter, a Psalm, and a Proverb.
Features

✅ Clickable checkboxes for every day
💾 Progress saves automatically to your browser
📊 Overall and per-segment progress tracking
🌿 Milestone reflections every 40 days

How to Use

Open the link in any browser on your phone or computer
Tap a day to mark it complete
Your progress saves automatically — no login needed
